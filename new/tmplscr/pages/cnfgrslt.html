Configure the Search Results Page

This is one of the best features Grepin Site Search offers you.
Configuring your search results page will give you the luxury of having the search results appear in a page that looks and feels like the other pages of your Web Site.

It will make your user feel that he/she is still browsing your Web Site.

You can configure the search results page in 2 ways

Basic Method

This is for those who are not comfortable with HTML.

In this method you will fill a form to the best of your knowledge and will let the automatic configuration do the rest.

Advanced Method
This is for those who are comfortable with HTML.

This gives you amazing flexibility to customize the search results page to look exactly the way you want it to look. In this method you will not fill any forms, but instead you will enter the HTML code itself in a text area and submit.

If you are comfortable with HTML, I recommend you use this method.
