Templates

Using the templates for the results page will give you the luxury of having the results appear in a page that looks and feels like a page out of your Web Site.
It will make your user feel that he/she is still browsing your Web Site.

Basic Method
Basic method is for those who are not comfortable with HTML.
In this method you will fill a form to the best of your knowledge and will let the automatic configuration do the rest.

Advanced Method
Advanced method is for those who are comfortable with HTML.
In this method you will not fill any forms, but instead you will enter the HTML code itself in a text area and submit.
If you are comfortable with HTML, this method is recommended.
